Phonotactics
=============

word : C? (Va F? C)* Vb F?

C (Consonant) : [w,t,p,s,l,f,h,k,ch,n,m]

Va (Vowel a) : [a, e  ,u, ai, eu , oi ]

Vb (Vowel b) : [i, o, io,  iu, ei, ia ]

F (Final) : [n,s,t] 

notes
----

Interpret the "Ê”" as "finish the vowel with your mouth in a 't' position"
