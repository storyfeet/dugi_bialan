zan Elenuna leli

# Little Redhood

lon don won la, li wei luga i zan leli min bi enon Elenuna

# Once there was a little girl called Elenuna

is-zan, li buna gin wei elein ei elein liawa bi ga-una esuno.

# She was good, and wore a bright red hood

zan Elenuna is-leli la, wei doimo lon bog man giasi ga-va-zo

# Elenuna, who we will call is-leli, lived beside a dark forest

esuno won la, ma min is-leli go "o da ma ma min ei delo beba dis. is-ma li belin egi" 

# one day, her mother told her "Oh [take] to granny this vegetable soup. She's feeling unwell"

"deso la, o va-wei va-zon ei lebo liawa bi man giasi dis : 
uno won la, o va-wei wega, lon don va-va, dan iasin
uno di la, o va-wei dugi, lon don va-va, gin vo-ezan eliasi"

# "but don't forget the rules of the forest:
# number 1 : don't stray from the path
# number 2: don't talk to wolves"

go "mi va" i is-leli, gin wei begin lon iasin.

# I won't she replied as she set off along the path.

giawin la is-leli wei munin.

# while going, she skipped

esuno wei esuno.  vo-wiasa wei ema-musi lon zewi.

# The sun shone, the birds sang in the sky

is-leli wei ema-musi egin.

# She (Elenuna) sang with them

deso la, vo-ezan eliasi vuli egi wei lugin ei is-leli. 

# However, a big bad wolf was watching her. 

is-ezan wei belin mugo ei is-leli. is-ezan wei bog ei is-leli 

# He wanted to eat her, he came besire her 

da is-leli go "vin da vemi"

# He spoke to her "where are you going?"

go "mi da ma ma min mi lon bog ziasi bi man giasi ei delo beba dis. is-ma li belin iagi"

# "I'm going to my granny on the other side of the woods with this vegetable soup. She's feeling sick"

is-leli wei bena lugin ei mugo delo da vo-ezan.

# She showed the soup to the wolf 

go "dan dis la, vin o megi-giawin. mi wei zon ei iasin leli" i vo-ezan wei dugi

# "You'd better be quick then, I know a short cut" said the wolf

is-ezan devin bena lugin ei iasin leli, wei giawin lon iasin begi

# He showed her the short cut, then went on his own way.

iasin is-ezan li egin da mengi bi ma ma min.

# His own way, led to grannies house

is-ezan wei ema egon lon luli mengi, gin gin ga-dugi leli ei go "mi li zi-leli vin, mi da vin ei delo beba" 

# He knocked on the door, and said in a small voice, "I'm your grandchild, I have vegetable soup for you.

go "o guma lon ensa, o zi-leli mi" 

# "O come inside my grandchild she said"

vo-ezan wei guma lon ensa, wei mugo ei is-ma gin belas una won.

# The wolf came inside, and ate up this granny in one mouthful

is-ezan wei elein ei elein is-ma, wei ensa ei man liabei is-ma, da wei lugin egin ei is-ma.

# He put on her clothes, and got into her bed in order to look like her.

zan Elenuna wei guma la, is-ezan li buna gein.

# When Elenuna arrived, he was ready.

is-leli go "mi li zi-leli min vin" wei dugi "mi da vin ei delo beba"

# "It's your granddaughter" she said, "I have brought you some vegetable soup"

go "o guma lon ensa" wei dugi i vo-ezan

# "O come in said the wolf"

is-leli wei giawin ensa wei lugin ei ma ma min is-leli. go "ba ma ma min mi. lugin vin li guma vuli mudei"

# She came inside and looked and her grandmother. "O granny, your eyes have gotten soo big"

go "li buna mudei da wei lugin ei vin"

# "This is very good so I can look at you."

go "ba ma ma min mi. gudei vin li guma vuli mudei"

# "Oh my granny. your ears have gotten soo big"

go "li buna mudei da wei gudei ei vin"

# "this is very good so I can hear you"

vo-ezan li dugi go "o guma lon bog mi, mi wei belin mugo leli ei mugo delo vin"

# The wolf said, "O come closer, I'd like to taste some of your soup"

is-leli wei guma da bog mudei, wei lugin bog mudei.

# She came closer, and looked closer.

go "ba ma ma min mi. gedin vin li wei guma vuli mudei"

# "O my granny. your teeth have gotten soo big"

go "li buna mudei da wei mugo ei vin"

# "This is very good, so I can EAT you"

vo-ezan wei giawin wiawa dan man liabei wei leban ei is-leli, wei mugo ei is-leli gin belas una won.

# The wolf leapt from the bed, got hold of Elenuna, and gobbled her up in one mouthful

beni la, is-ezan wei liabei, wei ema-liabei vuli

# Once he finished, he went to sleep, and snored

deso la, wei giawin lon bog mengi dis i zan bi din giasi 

# However, Walking beside this house was a woodcutter.

is-zan wei gudei ei ema-liabei wei esebin ei go "zan mezuni dis va-wei ema-liabei mudei egin dis"

# He heard the snoring and thought, "The old lady here doesn't snore this loud."

is-zan wei lugin lon luli lugin wei lugin ei vo-ezan bi wei liabei.

# He looked in the window, and saw a wolf sleeping.

is-zan wei ensa lon mengi, gin gin elo din is-begi wei din ei guli-vo-ezan da di

# he entered the house, and , using his axe cut the wolf in two.

dan guli-mugo wei zugo i zan Elenuna i ma ma min.

# Out from his guts came Elenuna and Granny.

is-muli wei lugin ei delo beba. 

# They all looked at the soup Elenuna had brought.

go "vin di wei va-wei esebin ei i delo beba dis wei belin ei mugo vo-vo", zan Elenuna wei dugi

# "Do you think this soup would like some meat", suggested Elenuna
