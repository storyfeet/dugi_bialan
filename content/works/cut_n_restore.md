# Cut and Restored Rope

da dis la, bei li o luga ei zan belin

# For this I need a volunteer

o zan buna. enon vin li vemi?

# Sir what's your name.

vin wei va wei luga ei lebo gein bi elo din?

# Do you have a scisors health and safety certificate?

-"wei"-

# "Yes"

lon buli emi dis la, li va-li buna ye bei wei guma din ei yawa vin dan guli-zo dan va-busi

# Do you mind if while we're performing this we accidently cut your head off?

-"li buna"-

# "That's ok"

buna, bei wei begin. bei wei luga ei lena, ei lesi emi (veli la li elo di din) .

# Ok, lets get started, here I have a rope and a magic wand (really its a pair of scissors).

o luga ei lesi emi dis.

# Please take hold of the magic wand.

o zan buna, ye egesa dis lon va-lon memuso di bi lena dis

# Sir, does this look to you to be about half way along the rope?

-"lon"-

# "yes"

buna. buna esi la, gin elo din o din ei lena lon man memuso dis. 

# Ok, if you could just take that pair of scissors and make a snip at that half way point.

buna, o da bei ei elo din. li da unis bei, o zon.

# Lovely, now if you could give back the scissors, for my safety you see.

lon begin la, bei wei luga ei gi lena. dis don la bei wei luga ei di lena. dan vemi man wei guma ei lena bi uno di. va-zan wei zon.

# We started with 1 piece of rope. Where did the second piece come from?  Nobody knows. 

deso la, bei wei dei gein. lena di dis wei duga gi esi la?

# But we can do more, what if we took these two ropes and turned them into one?

dis wei vei wega ei guli-ebin vin, wei vei wega ei yawa vin. vin wei ugin ei ye di lena wei guma gi lon enbin ugin vin.

# This is going to blow your minds, your heads will explode, as you see two ropes becoming one before your very eyes.

vin wei ba dei ebin yowa, va-wei gein zon ensa ei li ba dei yowa.

# You will be soooo amazed you won't be able to comprehend how awesome it is?

o ugin ei ye di lena wei guma gi lon enbin ugin vin. ba va-li dei buna dan muli ugin bi guvi vin. 

# Look ladies and gentlemen, two ropes into 1, right before your eyes. Isn't this the most amazing thing you have ever seen in your lives.

-"vin va-wei deso lugo"-

# "you just tied a knot"

dugi vemi ba, bei va-wei deso lugo.

# what do you mean I just tied a knot?

bei gin vemi wei gein bena ebin da vin ei ye lugo dis li lugo bi yasin emi

# what would it take to convince you that this knot is a magic knot.

bei wei zon ba. wei wega emi esi?

# I know, what if I make it disappear? 

o ugin buna, li wega

# watch closely, see it's gone.

ba ubei la, dis li meda yovi, li lon meni bi luga bei

# no, sorry that was just a joke, it was behind my hand. 

buna, wei vis. 

# Ok try again.

ba. va-wei deso yawin lon lena. li egin meda yovi.

# ah yes it just moved along the rope, just a joke.

lon dis don la veli veli 

# Ok, for real this time. (actually removes knot)

deso la, bei wei zon ei ye vin wei ebin ebin ei ye lugo lon vemi.

# But I'm sure you're wondering where the knot is.

-"lon vemi"-

# "where?"

lon luga bei

# It's in my hand.
